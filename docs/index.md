## What is BFT?

**Byzantine Fault Tolerance (BFT)** is the ability of a distributed system to reach consensus even when some nodes behave maliciously or fail arbitrarily.

## Why Study BFT?

BFT is fundamental to understanding:

- **Blockchain Technology**: Most blockchain consensus mechanisms are based on BFT principles
- **Distributed Systems**: Critical for building reliable distributed systems
- **Consensus Mechanisms**: Foundation for understanding how decentralized networks reach agreement
- **Fault Tolerance**: Essential knowledge for designing resilient systems
